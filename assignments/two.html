FREECODE CAMP

freeCodeCamp (also referred to as Free Code Camp) is a non-profit educational organization[4] that consists of an interactive learning web platform, an online community forum, chat rooms,online publications and local organizations that intend to make learning software development & computer programming accessible to anyone. Beginning with tutorials that introduce students to HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Python, C#, etc., students progress to project assignments that they complete either alone or in pairs.
